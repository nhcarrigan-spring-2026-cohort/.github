## What is the cohort?

This is a month-long event, taking place from February 2, 2026 to March 2, 2026. During this time, participants will be working on teams to develop real-world production-grade products. Participants will gain valuable experience working on a team, and get a sense of what it is like to work in a professional setting.

Most of the collaboration is coordinated through the [freeCodeCamp Discord community](https://discord.gg/KVUmVXA), so participants are expected to join and engage there!

## Do I need to have experience?

Nope! **The primary purpose of this event is to facilitate learning.** We won't deny people for having not learned things! That would defeat the point.

We ask about your experience and knowledge and such because we need to properly balance out the teams. We don't want a team of all new learners, for example, because that would not be a fun time!
